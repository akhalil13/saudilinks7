Is a high-ranking website directory submission really worth it?








Search engine optimization is one of the most important things you will learn. You need to submit your website to improve your search results. These sites may not have the right links, but they can provide them with the information you need. Let's find out.







Sites with pr6 and pr7 are common when you search high-ranking website directory submissions. This site is a good one to link to. Have you noticed something? This is the home page's pr ranking. Most of the links you are trying to get listed in have no pr. People make the biggest mistake of thinking that because the homepage has a high rank, it automatically carries over to the whole website. This is false. This page is the only one.

People pay money to have their website listed as a featured link.   دليل افضل المواقع    Consider what you are doing. This is how you are going to purchase a link from a page that has no ranking. This will not result in much traffic, if any. These people want submissions to these directories. They didn't go there to search for other websites. A directory link is only for SEO purposes. As long as your keyword is used, you will see your traffic increase in search engine results.

To get a free submission on website directories, you must place their link in your website. This is known as a reciprocal hyperlink. Although it may not be as powerful as a one-way link, you don't have to pay for a link without ranking. Most will ask you to link to their home pages. If your home page is well-ranked, your link may be more valuable than the one you receive in return. However, you will need to have more links in order to move up.

A website directory with a high page rank means that your site will be crawled quicker and will be found more quickly. Directories will want your home page URL. You should make sure to check in with them periodically to ensure that your link is still up on their site.

There are many website directories that allow submissions to pages within their site. These sites also have high page ranks. Websites like Domz directory and Yahoo directory are examples. They also have high page ranks on their inner pages. If you are able to list Domz, you will need to pay to be listed in Yahoo.  دليل المواقع   You can find other people with inner pages links if you search hard enough. They aren't that many.

Website directories are something I use all the time. Any link is better that none at all. Be aware of sites that require you to wait for submissions. They may be hoping you forget about them and they will get a one-way link for free. Don't listen to them if they say it will take several months for you to be listed. They want you to pay for a hyperlink. You should read the terms carefully as some may say that if your payment does not comply with their requirements, you will not receive any cash back.

When you submit a website directory, make sure to search for quality sites. Before you request a hyperlink, do some research. There is no reason to be in a poor directory.
